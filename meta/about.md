### Meet Erin Price

Erin Price writes children\'s, middle grade, and young adult fiction. Her young adult novel, *Walls*, is available on Amazon, and she is currently working on a new middle grade novel, *Lucky Me*, about a pair of quirky sisters.  Her stories and articles have been published in magazines such as the *Friend* and *LDS Living*.

Although she prefers to write for young people, Erin often writes for adults, too. She writes website content and blogs for small businesses around the world. She also recently won second place in the Loudoun County Library System\'s Write On! short story contest.

Erin has a degree in English with a minor in editing. She has taken many creative writing courses and attended writing conferences offered by the Society of Children\'s Book Writers and Editors (SCBWI) and Writing and Illustrating for Young Readers (WIFYR). Erin tells stories that lift and inspire young readers—stories that show readers that no matter how dark life may seem, there is always hope, light, and laughter available.
